About Me

I was born and raised in the state of Hawai`i, on the island of Kaua`i. I am currently still living on Kaua`i with my wife, our dog and four cats. I currently work as an IT support technician at IT Kaua`i. I am a Graphic Information Technology major with a focus on Full-Stack web development and a minor in technological management and entrepreneurship at Arizona State University. I am always open to new opportunities and willing to learn new things.
Some of my hobbies include photography and video games. I found my passion for coding as a teenager, designing MySpace pages for myself and my friends. Through ASU, I was able to attend a Tech coding bootcamp and have been learning a variety of coding languages. I aspire to one day create video games and applications that people will enjoy.
As I progress through school, I plan on continuing to build this site, adding more sections, pages, and completed projects that I have worked on. This will be my digital portfolio of my journey to becoming a Full-Stack Web Developer.
