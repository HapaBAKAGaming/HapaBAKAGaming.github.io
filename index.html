About Me

I was born and raised in the state of Hawai'i on the island of Kaua'i. I am currently still living on Kaua'i with my wife, our dog and, three cats. I currently work at Starbucks as a shift supervisor and I have been a partner for about five years. Some of my hobbies include going hiking with my wife, and playing video games (I also stream on twitch occasionally.) I also enjoy photography, the photos used in this webpage were taken by me.
After being out of school for about eight years, I decided it was finally time to pursue my dreams and go back to school. I am enrolled at Arizona State University through the SCAP program. I am a GIT major, aiming to "level up" to a full stack major. My goal after college is to work in the video game industry and design games for others to enjoy.
